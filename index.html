My Life Mission and Purpose

By Siva Prasad Mukherjee — Founder & Visionary, Banglay AI Shekho

Declaration

I, Siva Prasad Mukherjee, dedicate my life to one sacred goal — to make knowledge, technology, and education accessible to all, especially those who are limited by language or financial barriers.

Through my lifelong initiative, Banglay AI Shekho, I will build a self-sustaining ecosystem of education in Bengali, where learning is affordable, technology is friendly, and no learner is left behind because of fear or language.

I believe that Artificial Intelligence should be a tool of empowerment for every Bengali-speaking student, teacher, and guardian. My mission is to help them learn, teach, and grow in their own language — easily, confidently, and proudly.

My Work Will Focus On
  • Teaching through simplicity — bringing AI in clear Bengali.
  • Serving through sharing — offering free courses, e-books, GPT tools, and sessions.
  • Sustaining through modesty — keeping only small fees where needed to continue the mission.
  • Growing through research — improving pedagogy and technology.
  • Connecting through compassion — treating every learner as a friend.

"Education must never stop for lack of money, and no teacher should stop teaching for lack of support." Every rupee earned will help this mission reach more learners.

True education is what we share, true innovation is what we simplify, and true service is what we empower others to continue.

I pledge to spend the rest of my life advancing this mission — educating, empowering, and enlightening through the bridge between AI and Bengali education.

Serve first, earn only to serve more.
